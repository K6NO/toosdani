SCORPION
Category

is a four wheel bicycle made for my Bachelor thesis. This area of vehicle design is still fairly unexplored and can develop a lot. So I just kept main characteristics and came up with a couple of new ideas. Basic of my four wheel are the middle running frame and the trapezoid sitting arrangement. Thanks to this, the weight distribution is always ideal, unnecessary if 1, 2, 3 or 4 people are sitting inside. To make it more stable all passengers are in half-lying position and it has suspensions. Thanks to the short 180 cm wheelbase it has a great steering. The two outer seats on the side are easy to reach, optimal also for people with reduced mobility. To protect against sun or smaller rain it has a detachable root.
